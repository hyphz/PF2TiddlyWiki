Wildborn Magic

21st August 2020 at 10:56pm
Ancestry Elf Feat

This feat has specific setting requirements described in the Lost Omens Character Guide.

You have learned to access the old magic of wild places. Choose one cantrip from the Primal spell list.

You can cast this cantrip as an innate primal spell at will.

A cantrip is heightened to a spell level equal to half your level rounded up.

Cantrips available:

  • Acid Splash
  • Dancing Lights
  • Detect Magic
  • Disrupt Undead
  • Electric Arc
  • Guidance
  • Know Direction
  • Light
  • Prestigitation
  • Produce Flame
  • Ray of Frost
  • Read Aura
  • Sigil
  • Stabilize
  • Tanglefoot
