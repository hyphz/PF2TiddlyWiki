You touch a creature and force them to see and feel the ancestors surrounding you.

The target takes 1d4 Mental damage, with results depending on a Will save.

Heightened (+1) The mental damage increases by 1d4.

Critical SuccessThe target is unaffected.
SuccessThe target takes half damage.
FailureThe target is Frightened 1 and takes full damage.
Critical FailureThe target is Frightened 2 and takes double damage.
