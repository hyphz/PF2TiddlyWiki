Elfbane HobgoblinYou gain the Resist Elf Magic reaction. (This resists all magic, not just that of elves.)
Runtboss HobgoblinYou gain the Group Coercion skill feat. If you roll a success on an Intimidation check to Coerce a goblin (but not other creatures with the goblin trait), you get a critical success instead; if you roll a critical failure, you get a failure instead.
Smokeworker HobgoblinYou gain fire resistance equal to half your level (minimum 1). You automatically succeed at the DC 5 flat check to target a Concealed creature if that creature is concealed only by smoke.
Warmarch HobgoblinIf you fail, but not critically fail, to Subsist in the wilderness, you can still keep yourself fed with poor meals. When exploring, you can Hustle twice as long before you have to stop.
Warrenbred HobgoblinWhen you are underground, you can use the Seek action to sense undetected creatures within a 30-foot burst instead of a 15-foot burst. In addition, if you roll a success on an Acrobatics check to Squeeze, you get a critical success instead.
