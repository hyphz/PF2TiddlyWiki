Tomb-Watcher's Glare

19th August 2020 at 8:29pm
Ancestry Divine Dwarf Feat

This feat has specific setting requirements described in the Lost Omens Character Guide.

When you critically hit an Undead creature, or an undead creature critically fails a saving throw against one of your abilities, you drive your divine wrath home in your enemy’s heart.

The undead is Enfeebled 1 for 1 round.
