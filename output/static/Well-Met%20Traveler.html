Well-Met Traveler

23rd August 2020 at 5:24pm
Ancestry Catfolk Feat

You have seen people from so many walks of life in your travels that you naturally adopt a pleasant and affable demeanor when meeting others.

You are trained in Diplomacy. If you would automatically become trained in Diplomacy (from your background or class, for example), you instead become trained in a skill of your choice.

In addition, you gain the Hobnobber skill feat.
