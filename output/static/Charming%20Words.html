Charming Words

11th August 2020 at 1:25am
Auditory Concentrate Enchantment Focus Spell Incapacitation Linguistic Mental Spell Uncommon Verbal Wizard

Level1
Components Verbal 1 Focus Point
Cast Time
Range30 feet
Area
Target1 creature
Save Will
DurationUntil the start of your next turn

You whisper enchanting words to deflect your foe’s ire.

The target must attempt a Will save.

Incapacitation effect: All targets of more than twice the spell level use the result one better than the save they rolled.

Critical SuccessThe target is unaffected.
SuccessThe target takes a -1 circumstance penalty to attack rolls and damage rolls against you.
FailureThe target can't use Hostile Actions against you.
Critical FailureThe target is Stunned 1 and can't use Hostile Actions against you.
