Half-Elf

29th August 2020 at 12:22am
Heritage Versatile

Although this heritage follows the versatile heritage rules, you must be a human to take it.

You gain the Elf trait and Low‑Light Vision.

In addition, you can select Elf , Half-Elf , and Human feats whenever you gain an ancestry feat.
