Save

A Will save is used to avoid being tricked, being afraid, or other mental effects.

Will is governed by Wisdom.

Being Stupefied, Fatigued, Sickened or Frightened reduces your Will rolls.

This is used to defend against:

  • Coerce
  • Command an Animal
  • Demoralize
  • Fascinating Performance
  • Frightful Presence
  • Make an Impression
  • Scare to Death

And to save against these spells:

  • Anathematic Reprisal
  • Animal Vision
  • Bane
  • Banishment
  • Calm Emotions
  • Charm
  • Circle of Protection
  • Cloak of Colors
  • Collective Transposition
  • Command
  • Confusion
  • Crisis of Faith
  • Crusade
  • Crushing Despair
  • Daze
  • Death Knell
  • Dimensional Anchor
  • Divine Aura
  • Dominate
  • Enthrall
  • Fabricated Truth
  • Fear
  • Feeblemind
  • Hallucination
  • Hideous Laughter
  • Hypnotic Pattern
